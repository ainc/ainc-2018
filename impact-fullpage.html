The World’s First Real-Time Substance Use Treatment Locator Site

The Project

Find Help Now (findhelpnow.org) is creating a faster and more personalized way for people to access treatment for substance use disorders (SUD). After an exhaustive search for the right partner, the Find Help Now team chose APAX Software to design and develop a new responsive website that modernizes and expedites the search for substance use treatment and recovery options. APAX partnered with the Kentucky Injury Prevention and Research Center (KIPRC) to build a highly responsive, mobile-friendly, easy-to-use, section 508 compliant website to facilitate access to SUD treatment centers. Near real-time availability of SUD treatment openings drastically reduces the time spent by health care professionals, families, and individuals seeking treatment programs. Users can filter facilities based on location, facility type, gender identity, the type of treatment needed, and more.

The Outcome

Within one year, the Find Help Now site reduced the time to find treatment and recovery options from 72 hours to 6 minutes and had 253,803 visitors from all 50 states. In addition, the Find Help Now model is now being implemented by 14 other states across the US.

Omni Life

A Fellowship Company, OmniLife, began the program in April of 2019. Since then, the company and its founders have developed a relationship with Awesome Inc employees and members. After Demo Day of our Spring ‘19 Bootcamp, OmniLife hired two of our graduates. 2019 Highlights

Startup Weekend

For the 12th year, Awesome Inc facilitated Startup Weekend. During this weekend, community members join teams to grow an idea into a startup in 54 hours. It's high-energy, intense, and one of our favorite community-building events. Often during Startup Weekend, teams create surveys to get feedback on their ideas. This year, the Startup Lex Slack channel became an easy and effective way not only to get feedback, but also to begin conversations between strangers and facilitate long-term partnerships working together toward the same goal - innovation and creativity in the Bluegrass!

Women Innovators and Founders of Lexington

The goal of Startup Lex is to nurture organic growth of the community. This year during Global Entrepreneurship Week, three women in the startup community identified a gap in the ecosystem that they decided to fill: a consistent place for women to connect in the Lexington startup community. So during GEW, they hosted the first meetup downtown, attracting over 30 women. This initiative exemplifies the goal of Startup Lex: to create an accessible community, facilitate connections among community members, and share the information so that groups like this can continue to organically form and grow to serve founders.

Team Alpha

Our internship program, Team Alpha, is an integral part of Awesome Inc's mission of making Lexington a better place to live and work. We bring on passionate individuals who are hard working and not afraid to fail. Interns produce compelling videos to tell stories that matter, create web applications that improve processes and efficiency, build community by bringing people together through our communication channels, and design visual graphics that keep all of the above creative and cohesive.
