School

Helping educate the youth

We are here to help your student's education

Students are facing a challenging and unique year in education. Don't let them do it alone. We are creating a community for students around the area to learn together in our space while following the COVID guidelines.

Why Choose Awesome Inc U?

Awesome Inc has been teaching students since 2013. Our team has taught over 1,000 youth students with a combined teaching experience of over 14,000 hours. Our team is eager to help students learn together during this unique year.

COVID Safety Guidelines

Our space is set up well to allow us to follow these health safety measures:

  • Classes will be capped at 10 students to ensure we can distance the students around the room
  • Our team will be conducting cleanings before and after the students arrive for the camp each day.
  • Temperature checks will be conducted at our discretion. Students with temperatures greater than 100.4 degrees Fahrenheit will not be permitted to join us that day, and individuals must be fever-free without using medicine to reduce the fever for at least 24 hours.
  • We will require masks to be worn except for when students are at their own desk without an instructor within 6 feet of them.
How does it work?

Students can be dropped off at Awesome Inc at 8am and can be picked up anytime before 4pm. Depending on your student's current academic level and NTI work from school, our instructional team will work 1-on-1 with your student to ensure your student is not only progressing, but excelling at their academics.
