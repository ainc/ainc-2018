Why We're Awesome

Team Alpha is a group of about 20 talented interns that learn, work, and have fun with each other to help make Awesome Inc, and our community, a better place to live and work. There are no age or time restrictions, we just require that you follow our core values.

Team Alpha's captain, Emily, is way more fun than any other boss you'll ever have. Our Video Team gets to hang out with millionaires, like the Dippin' Dots inventor. Our Development Team gets to work on sweet mobile apps and code websites, such as EntrepreneurHOF.com. The Events & Marketing Team help plan fun ways to build community, while pushing out creative marketing strategies. And the Graphic Design Team gets to create everything that all of our teams and initiatives use like flyers, social media images, and more!

Who we're looking for

We're looking for anyone who wants to have fun and follow Rule #4: "Always do something awesome over something not awesome." Our primary needs are for people interested in video, web development, and graphic design. We also need event coordinators, social media whizzes, marketing, and PR people, as well as writers for our blogs.

Why Join?

In addition to the free t-shirt, Team Alpha provides you with an opportunity to improve your skills, portfolio and network. Our goal is to assure you the best experience by helping you achieve your goals whether that is getting into your dream school or working for your favorite company. Also, if you're good enough, you can get a sweet nickname like Melo, Cool, Fresh, Rush, and Nerf did.
